Frequently Asked Questions

Preise & Konditionen

Why does every project start with an assessment? ▼

Because we don't want to build a solution that misses your real processes. The assessment is simultaneously analysis, risk check, and planning foundation – preventing costly mistakes. The fixed price gives you certainty before larger budgets are committed.

Can I credit the assessment against a later project? ▼

Yes. If you commission an implementation project within 60 days of the assessment, we credit 50% of the assessment price against the project budget.

What are the payment terms? ▼

For assessments: 50% on order, 50% on completion. For larger projects: milestone-based billing in 3–4 tranches. Retainer: monthly in advance, cancellable with 4 weeks notice.

What happens if the project costs more than planned? ▼

For fixed-price projects: nothing. The agreed price stands. If scope changes, we discuss it openly – no hidden hours, no retrospective surcharges. That's why we always start with a careful assessment.

Do you work with companies outside Baden-Baden? ▼

Yes, throughout Germany and the DACH region. Assessments can be conducted fully remotely. For on-site appointments we calculate travel costs separately – no flat fees.

Can't find the right package?

Every company is different. In a 30-minute call we'll find out together what makes sense for you – no sales pressure.
